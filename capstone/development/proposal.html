Capstone Proposal

Topic

My topic will be on AI's involvement in health and health research. AI's ability for pattern recognition using large data sets promises advancements for health and medicine in the near future. This idea will rely on speculative design and utopian/dystopian narratives. To portray this idea, I will need to speculate possible futures with a reliance on AI in healthcare and explore the possible effects it has on our society through a utopian/dystopian lens.

Possible Futures

I think the idea will rely on input from users to collect data on how people currently trust and feel about AI with its involvement in medicine/healthcare. The feedback for the questions, likely at the end, will display data on how AI is being used for medicine research and its accuracy. To collect data, I would use the back4app and d3 to visualize data on the page. In terms of visuals, I think it would be interesting to present the narrative as a dystopian future, but becomes a middle “utopia” when the data is presented. This approach would allow for a more realistic look into the future of AI and health, while relying on input from users to decide its route.

Some concerns for this topic/idea would be navigating biases. I want to present a future where AI could be used to help humanity, rather than its downfall. Most wouldn’t argue that AI can be relied on for analyzing data and its potential for medicine/disease research. I think I could go two different directions with the project, focusing on AI’s involvement in research or how it will involve itself with real patients and providing diagnoses.

Target Audience

My target audience would be the younger generation who are opposed to the use of AI. I want to convince the audience of a positive or neutral future with AI. The audience’s perspective should shift or reconsider how AI can be implemented within our jobs, not just in the healthcare field.

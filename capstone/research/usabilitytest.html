Userbility Results

Observations

  • Users had difficulty navigating the page. Scrolling to the next box was not intuitive after each submission click.
  • Users were surprised by the current advancements of AI in medicine. The data and questions helped to provide evidence/ideas on how AI can transform healthcare.
  • Charts need additional titles to clarify measurements and communicate data. They should additionally include its corresponding answer choice to communicate to the user effectively the intended message.

Improvements

I need to reconsider how to implement scrolling on my page. Importing GSAP is being considered to automatically scroll to the next question, since most users expected to be prompted to the next question. Additionally, charts and other data visualizations need to be added to appropriately convey data and arguments. To finalize the design, graphics that communicate the topic of health and AI will be added to the page. To conclude the narrative, a collective result will display past users' answers.

Navigation/Interactions

Users had difficulty navigating to the next question. Users understood to scroll after interacting with the page. Buttons and hover features worked properly and identified to the user what part of the page they could interact with.

Impact

The project is able to effectively communicate my intended message. Users shared they were unaware and surprised by how AI is currently being used in healthcare and its potential impact in the years to come. Questions were intentionally poised to make the user consider the questions thoughtfully.

Modifications

The inclusion of charts, maps, and graphs need to be added for data visualization. Relevant graphics to communicate the topic will be added throughout the page. A scroll bar that allows users to know how many questions they need to answer and how close they are to completing the forms. The collection of user inputs to be displayed at the end of the page needs to be styled and finalized to make the narrative complete.

Feedback

I plan to change and modify my page based on user feedback. Adjusting the scroll will be my top priority for users to efficiently navigate the page. I will use GSAP for automatic scrolling for users to click to the next question. I will add the percentages of user inputs for the user to read and contemplate what the general misunderstandings surrounding AI currently are. The final change I will implement is graphics for visual clarity and communication of topic.
